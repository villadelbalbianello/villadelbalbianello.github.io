How to get

On foot
You can reach the Villa on foot from Lenno. It is 1 km away with an uphill stretch that can be traveled in about 25 minutes.

From the lake
It is possible to reach the Villa via the lake by taking advantage of the taxiboat services with fee from the Lido di Lenno.

By bus
From Como to Lenno, bus line SPT C10. Lenno stop, then continue on foot or in taxiboat. Follow the signs from Piazza della Chiesa and Lido di Lenno.

By car
Reach the Lenno Parking lot, in via degli Artigiani, then continue on foot or in taxiboat.

On the train
With the Trenord trains, Milan branch: get off at the Como Lago station. For train timetables see the Trenord website.
20% discount on the admission ticket, for holders of a valid ITINERO or IOVIAGGIO card.
10% discount on the admission ticket, for holders of an ordinary Trenord ticket validated on the same day of the visit with destination to the FAI headquarters site served by Trenord railway transport. Specifically: Como.

Accessibility

Disabled visitors have free access to the FAI assets open to the public by presenting the ANMIC card - National Association of Mutilated and Civil Disabled.

The villa is partially accessible. In the event of walking problems, contact the Villa for more information.
